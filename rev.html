Story

Rev is an online platform that specializes in providing a range of language-related services. Primarily known for its transcription services, Rev.com offers accurate and efficient conversion of audio and video content into written text. The platform also extends its services to include professional translation and captioning, catering to a diverse clientele that includes businesses, researchers, and individuals. Rev.com is recognized for its user-friendly interface, quick turnaround times, and a pool of skilled freelancers who contribute to the high-quality and reliable services offered. Overall, Rev.com stands out as a reliable solution for transcription, translation, and captioning needs in the digital landscape

My Role

As the Senior Visual Designer at Rev, I played a pivotal role within the dynamic creative team. My responsibilities spanned a spectrum of creative endeavors, including the conception and crafting of captivating website imagery, the development of impactful marketing assets, the creation of visually engaging illustrations, and the dynamic realm of motion graphics. As a key contributor to the creative vision at Rev, I continually elevated the visual identity and brand presence through a diverse range of design elements.

Learning Content Infographic

This visually captivating infographic, designed in a collage style with a photomontage that brings to life the essence of a professor's messy desk. This infographic serves as a compelling showcase, presenting the responses of over 1,000 students from various colleges and universities across the United States.

Delving into the realm of accessibility, it highlights the students' perspectives on how and why they utilize captions, subtitles, and transcripts. The vibrant and dynamic design captures the diversity of experiences and provides valuable insights into the importance and impact of these accessibility features in educational settings.

Whether it's enhancing comprehension, facilitating note-taking, or supporting students with different learning preferences, this infographic presents a comprehensive overview of the benefits that captions, subtitles, and transcripts bring to the educational landscape.
